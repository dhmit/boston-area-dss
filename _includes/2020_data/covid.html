Postponement of 2020 Symposium

Given the current circumstances surrounding COVID-19, we are postponing the Boston-Area Digital Scholarship Symposium 2020, originally scheduled for Friday, May 1, 2020 at MIT.

As soon as we are able to resume planning for next Spring, we will be in touch with information about further details for #BDSS2021.

Effective immediately, we are closing our call for posters. We have already received a number of excellent poster submissions, and we have reached out to those authors with next steps. Invited speakers will also be contacted.
